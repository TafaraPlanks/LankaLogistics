About LKL

Your trusted logistics partner

Founded in 2008, Lanka Logistics (LKL) has grown to become one of the most reliable trucking companies in the region. We specialize in both perishable and non-perishable goods transportation, serving businesses of all sizes.

Our mission is to provide safe, efficient, and cost-effective transportation solutions while maintaining the highest standards of service and reliability.

Modern, well-maintained fleet
